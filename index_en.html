1. Self Introduction

Born in Osaka in 1987, lived in Osaka until completing a master's degree.

Participated in the summer internship at NTT Communication Science Laboratories (Atsugi, Kanagawa), admired the free atmosphere and high research level, and decided to join NTT.

After joining NTT, engaged in mobile phone sales training at NTT Docomo Kyushu (Fukuoka City, Fukuoka Prefecture) for six months. Later started full-scale research activities at NTT Yokosuka Research and Development Center (Yokosuka City, Kanagawa Prefecture).

Since April 2023, a doctoral student at the Institute of Science Tokyo (formerly Tokyo Institute of Technology). Member of IEEE and IEICE.
